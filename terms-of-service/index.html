1. What Buoy is

Buoy is a hyperlocal, real-time coordination utility for dog owners. It answers one question — "who is at my dog park right now, and do they want to hang out?" — by letting you check in at a park, see who else is currently present, and send a one-tap invite to meet. Buoy treats the park as the social object. It is deliberately not a feed, a messaging app, a dating service, a training service, or a pet-care marketplace.
